# Bio

## Currently working on the two following projects:
- **Himmel's Ending** - 
  This is a turn based roguelike with combat similar to games like Baldur's Gate. Your goal is to reach reach the highest chapter (each chapter is an event in the game) in Himmels adventure. Do this by: unlocking new characters, aquire items, abilities, spells and buffs. 
- **GemForge** - 
  Action roguelike with infinite amount of possibilities! Fight waves of enemies with your self crafted attacks and spells. Combine gems and crystals to craft your very own spell or attack and unleash it upon the endless enemies!
